Greetings and Welcome


Thank you for visiting us today. Grace Life Conference is here again. It's that time of the year for our convergence in the power of God's Spirit.

I hope you're you getting ready also.

This has always been one of my most anticipated meetings of the year and still is and as ALWAYS, we have great hopes and expectations in the Lord for these meetings this year. We are certain of the release of blessings over and upon all in the Teaching of the Gospel and the Ministration of the things of God's Spirit boldly by us.

We're certain in the Lord that the hungry will be fed, the thirsty will have their longings drenched by life in the Spirit.

Those who desire to be on the track of a genuine relationship with God will be set on the path of divine experience by His Word.

Yes! Those who hunger, Come! Those who are thirsty, Come! Those who desire to be set on the track of a genuine relationship with God, we'd love to have you!

Come with an expectant heart!
Come with a desire to learn!
Come with a pen and a paper!

It's the WORD by the SPIRIT. It's the SUPERNATURAL life experience. It's JESUS glorified!

Come! Tell a friend!

Jerr-Alex Abolaji
